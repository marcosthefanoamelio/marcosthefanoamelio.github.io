Research Interests

My main interest lies within Geometric Group Theory. More concretely, the goal of my PhD project was to constuct non-split sharply 2-transitive groups of odd characteristic (see the preprint for the result). In the process of the construction, I became interested in geometric small cancellation and thus also in groups acting on Gromov-hyperbolic spaces . Lately, I have also developed an interest on the Boone-Higman conjecture and in the (closely related) twisted Brin-Thompson groups .
